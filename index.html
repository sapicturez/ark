Service 01 — Water-based therapy

Hydrotherapy

Hydrotherapy harnesses the unique properties of water to improve mobility and overall health. Buoyancy and resistance combine to strengthen and condition muscles without stressing joints — and warm water at 29–30°C dilates blood vessels, easing aches and aiding healing.

How it works

Buoyancy minimises weight-bearing stress on joints, helping prevent and manage tissue injury, inflammation, and pain. Water also gives sensory feedback that builds body awareness. Resistance — adjusted via belt speed, water height, incline, and jets — improves strength and stamina.

Conditions it supports

Weak muscles Underdeveloped muscles Osteoarthritis Hip dysplasia Patella luxation IVDD Paralysed & three-legged dogs

Service 02 — Photobiomodulation

Laser Therapy

Laser therapy reduces pain, accelerates healing, and lowers inflammation. Pets dealing with pain, swelling, or slow-healing wounds can benefit from focused laser treatment.

How it works

Therapy laser light stimulates the mitochondria within cells, triggering a beneficial cascade that accelerates blood flow, heals tissue, and reduces inflammation. The laser also encourages the release of endorphins, enhancing nerve regeneration and easing the perception of pain.

Conditions it supports

Senior arthritis Muscle / tendon strain Cruciate ligament rupture Back pain Nerve pain Non-healing wounds Laser acupuncture

Service 03 — Electrotherapy

TENS & NMES

Both forms of electrotherapy target nerves through a mild electrical current. TENS provides pain relief; NMES treats muscular weakness — together, they're powerful tools in recovery.

How it works

A small battery-operated device delivers gentle current through electrodes placed on the treatment area. The signals reach nerves and muscles, creating a tingling sensation or controlled contraction that supports recovery.

Conditions it supports

Arthritis Post-surgical pain Chronic pain Muscle atrophy prevention Nerve injury recovery Improved circulation Weakness & paralysis

Service 04 — TCM & modern integration

Acupuncture

Acupuncture is the gentle insertion of fine, sterile needles into specific points to bring positive effects on the nervous and physiological systems — pain modulation, circulation, and balance.

How it works

Acupuncture stimulates peripheral nerves, sending signals to the central nervous system to modulate pain and trigger the release of endorphins and neurotransmitters such as serotonin. It enhances blood flow, regulates cytokine activity to reduce inflammation, and balances autonomic function.

Conditions it supports

Arthritis Hip dysplasia Cruciate injury IVDD Paralysis Weak hind legs IBD Liver / kidney support

Service 06 — Food as medicine

Nutrition & Wellness

A healthy body starts with proper nutrition. We blend modern science with Traditional Chinese Medicine, treating food as medicine that supports growth, immunity, and emotional balance.

The five food elements

TCM recognises five food elements that support specific organs and overall balance: Wood (liver, digestion), Fire (heart, mind, circulation), Earth (spleen, energy), Metal (lungs, immunity), Water (kidneys, fluids). Ingredients are chosen by their TCM pattern, and customised formulations follow AAFCO standards. We also incorporate gut microbiome and vitamin/mineral testing.

Conditions it supports

Obesity Muscle & bone strength Skin allergies Inflammatory bowel Food sensitivities Chronic pancreatitis Liver disorders Cancer support
